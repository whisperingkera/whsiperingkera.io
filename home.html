Welcome to WHISPERING KERA Ecological Homestay

Attupuram, Poovar Island Area, Kerala

Your best comfort, with the smallest possible ecological footprint

Located at the Poovar Island site, WHISPERING KERA Guest House offers you privileged access to southern « God’s Own Country » KERALA in India.

At the extreme south of Kerala, on the border with Tamil Nadu, the site of Poovar is well-known for its network of Backwaters that flows into

the Laccadive Sea and attracts large numbers of tourists from Europe, USA and India. The area has been known for a long time thanks to the nearby

tourist resort of Kovalam, as well as the various ayurvedic treatments one can find in the region.
